Privacy Policy – The Ear of the Closer GPT

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Overview

The Ear of the Closer (“the GPT”) is an AI-powered conversational assistant designed to help users respond to sales objections and close conversations more effectively. This GPT operates entirely within the ChatGPT platform and does not independently collect, store, or process personal data outside of what is provided during an active session.

2. Information Users May Provide

When using the GPT, users may voluntarily input information such as:

  • Sales objections or statements from prospects
  • Hypothetical or real sales scenarios
  • Non-sensitive business or financial discussion details

Users should not enter sensitive personal information, including but not limited to Social Security numbers, full financial account numbers, medical records, or login credentials.

3. How Information Is Used

All information entered is used solely to generate real-time objection rebuttals and closing language within the current conversation.

The GPT does not save conversation data, build user profiles, track individuals, or retain memory across sessions.

4. Data Storage & Retention

The Ear of the Closer GPT does not store or retain user data. Any data processing occurs transiently during the active session and is governed by OpenAI’s platform-level policies.

5. Data Sharing

The GPT does not share user-provided information with third parties. All interactions remain within the ChatGPT environment and are subject to OpenAI’s privacy and security controls.

6. Training & Model Improvement

User conversations are not used by this GPT for training or model improvement. Any platform-level data handling is governed solely by OpenAI’s privacy practices.

7. Financial & Insurance Context Disclaimer

The GPT may generate language related to insurance, financial services, or estate planning conversations. All outputs are for educational and conversational support only and should not be considered legal, financial, or insurance advice.

8. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations, using generated responses ethically and appropriately, and avoiding the entry of sensitive or regulated personal data.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in functionality or compliance requirements. Continued use of the GPT constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
